What Is a CCTV Cable?

A CCTV cable is specially designed for transmitting video signals, power, and sometimes audio between CCTV cameras and recording devices.

  • Designed for continuous video transmission
  • Low signal loss over long distances
  • Strong shielding against interference
  • Suitable for indoor and outdoor use

What Is a Normal Cable?

Normal cables are general-purpose electrical wires used mainly for power supply, lighting, and household appliances. They are not designed to carry video signals.

  • No video signal support
  • Higher interference
  • Poor durability for CCTV use

Why Using CCTV Cable Is Important

Using proper CCTV cables ensures clear video quality, stable signal transmission, lower maintenance cost, and longer system life. For commercial and industrial security systems, the right cable is essential.
